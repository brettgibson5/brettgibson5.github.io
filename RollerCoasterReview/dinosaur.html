REIVEW

If you've been on this ride or look below at the picture, you can clearly see that this is not a roller coaster. But this ride is too fun to pass up, so I'm going to review it anyways. This is the perfect example of a true Disney ride. Although it’s not as fast or as tall as a ride at Cedar Point or Six Flags, it captures you through a story where you have to save a dinosaur by traveling back in time before the asteroid hits the earth. The dinosaurs are also very lifelike which adds to the thrill. The vehicle is always bobbing around and trying to avoid the next dinosaur. Just a fun themed ride that will get your heart racing.
