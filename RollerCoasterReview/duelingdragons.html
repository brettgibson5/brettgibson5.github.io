DUELING DRAGONS

<<< back to ISLANDS OF ADVENTURE

SPECS

Max Height: 125 ft.
Max Speed: 60 mph (fire), 55 mph (ice)
Inversions: 5
Duration: 2 min. 25 sec.
Manufacturer: Bolliger & Mabillard

REIVEW

Although I couldn't tell you the difference between the ‘Fire’ and ‘Ice’ versions, I do remember that they are fun. The line is also very nicely themed, combining a Disney-style theme with a Six Flags ride. These 2 tracks are fast and very enjoyable, essentially a longer version of Batman at Six Flags Great America. Obviously, the best part is where you almost collide with the other car when they both pass through the loop (see picture).
