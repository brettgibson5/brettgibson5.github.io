REIVEW

This new coaster in Animal Kingdom is a great one. Disney has come a long way from Thunder Mountain and Matterhorn with this ride. Everest has you going inside a massive mountain Disney made from concrete, similar to the Matterhorn but much larger. As you travel up an incline through the mountain, you find that the track ahead is completely destroyed by the yeti. All of a sudden you stop by some brakes and then free fall backwards. While you were stopped, the track was switched so that you will be going through a different part backwards. You will then meet up with the yeti, who is a huge animatronic but I was convinced that it was real. A great coaster from Disney with a signature theme, my only wish it were a bit longer. But all in all, a great coaster from Disney.
