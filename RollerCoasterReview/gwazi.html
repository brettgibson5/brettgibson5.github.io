REIVEW

Gwazi is a fun racing wooden coaster, I think. I barely remember it (not a good sign), but I recall it being a bit bumpier than I would have liked. Still it's always fun when you are racing against another train and feel like you have something to do with how fast you go. Decent, but I would much rather go on Viper.
