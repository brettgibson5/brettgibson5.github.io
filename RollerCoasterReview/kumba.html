SPECS

Max Height: 143 ft.
Max Speed: 60 mph
Inversions: 7
Duration: 2 min. 54 sec.
Manufacturer: Bolliger & Mabillard

REIVEW

When I was at Busch Gardens last year, I had really high expectations for SheiKra and none for Kumba. I loved Kumba and thought SheiKra was just alright. Kumba set the industry standard for steel twisters, featuring a dive roll and hard banks. This is one of those rides that you could ride over and over again. Created by Switzerland's B&M, they create some of the greatest rides on the planet.
