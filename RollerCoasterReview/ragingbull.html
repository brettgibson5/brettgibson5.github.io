RAGING BULL

<<< back to SIX FLAGS GREAT AMERICA

SPECS

Max Height: 202 ft.
Max Speed: 73 mph
Inversions: 0
Duration: 2 min. 30 sec.
Manufacturer: Bolliger & Mabillard

REIVEW

Raging Bull is the ultimate ride at Six Flags Great America. Considering I grew up close to this park, I have a bias to this ride. But B&M weren’t messing around when they made this hyper coaster. It features a ton of drops and huge banks, and is just a lot of fun to ride. With seat restraints that give you tons of freedom, that first drop feels like base jumping. This one will be on the top coasters list for a while.
