SHEIKIRA

<<< back to BUSCH GARDENS

SPECS

Max Height: 200 ft.
Max Speed: 70 mph
Inversions: 1
Duration: 2 min. 20 sec.
Manufacturer: Bolliger & Mabillard

REIVEW

I was really excited to ride SheiKra because I had never been on anything like it. This ride does feature a completely vertical drop, which was a thrill. The ride was somewhat fun, but nothing really memorable. I do remember you cross some water and the train splashes some water out of the back. I felt that was such a waste because no one on the ride knew what just happened, but it does look pretty cool for the people watching the ride. Overall I would say fun, but could have been better.
