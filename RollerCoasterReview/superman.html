REIVEW

Before riding Superman: Ultimate Flight, I’ve been on X-Flight a few times (the former Six Flags Worlds of Adventure). I say this because I loved X-Flight and thought Superman would continue that passion for the new flying ride. Wrong. Don’t get me wrong, this ride is fun to go on, and I suggest you do if you are at Great America. But I have a problem with it. It is too short. It has a drop, and awesome pretzel loop, and a few turns, and barrel roll, and then you’re done. The pretzel loop is an awesome experience though, you really feel the gravity pull against you. Overall, this ride could have been better, it’s alright though.
