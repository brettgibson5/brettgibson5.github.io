REIVEW

Tower of Terror is just a glorified freefall drop thrill ride, but it is so well done. The setting is a 1920's hotel that gets struck by lightining and now you must go up the elevator. Little do we know is that a 13 story plunge is our immediate future. The story is top notch and you really get involved, similar to a Splash Mountain or Dinosaur. The ride also brings you up and down numerous times, so it’s worth the wait.
